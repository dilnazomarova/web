Why are photos so important to us in text messages?

In social networks, a popular feature now is to use images in correspondence with private or group chats.

It all became widespread due to the fact that, through the picture, you can convey emotions or attitudes to a particular person as a whole.

Examples of using images in messengers will be shown below.

Pictures have different meanings. They can be used as:

  • gratitude
  • forgiveness
  • greetings
  • farewell
